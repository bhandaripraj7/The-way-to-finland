Courses

There are many courses and programs that international students can study in Finland. Here are some examples:

Bachelor's and Master's Degrees

Finland is home to many universities that offer degree programs in various fields, including engineering, business, arts, sciences, and more. Some popular universities for international students include the University of Helsinki, Aalto University, and the University of Turku.

Language Courses

If you're interested in learning Finnish or Swedish, you can find language courses offered by universities, private language schools, and other institutions throughout Finland.

Summer Schools

Many universities in Finland offer summer schools that are open to international students. These programs often focus on specific topics or disciplines and provide an opportunity to study and network with students from around the world.

Vocational Education and Training

Finland has a strong vocational education and training system that is highly regarded around the world. International students can study a range of subjects, including hospitality, tourism, health care, and more.

Online Courses

If you're unable to travel to Finland, there are many online courses and programs that you can take from anywhere in the world. Some examples include the online courses offered by the University of Helsinki's MOOC platform and the online courses offered by the Finnish Virtual University.

Our Facilities

Finland is known for its excellent education system, including its colleges and universities. Overall, Finnish colleges and universities provide a wide range of facilities and services to support student learning, development, and wellbeing.

World Class Library

Finnish colleges and universities have excellent library facilities with access to a vast range of books, journals, and online resources.

Sports Facilities

Finnish universities offer a variety of sports facilities, including basketball, gyms, swimming pools, and sports fields.

Cafeteria

Finnish universities have on-campus cafeterias where students can purchase meals and snacks at affordable prices.
